yeonubot is a Discord community-management bot for VALORANT-focused gaming communities. It verifies members' Riot accounts, assigns Discord roles matching their competitive tier, and keeps them up to date automatically — so scrims and custom matches are organized around real, verified skill.

What yeonubot does

커뮤니티 운영의 전 과정을 한 봇으로.

🏅Verified rank roles

Members verify their Riot account; the bot assigns the matching tier role and shows current act, previous act, and peak tier on their community profile. Tiers refresh automatically on a schedule.

📘Guided onboarding

New members complete a guided intro (rules & profile) before receiving access, with staff review for approvals.

⚔️Scrim-ready organization

Role panels for games, servers, and tiers make it easy to find teammates of similar skill for scrims and custom matches — the core activity of the community.

📈Activity & economy

Chat/voice activity levels and a community currency reward participation.

🛡️Moderation & logs

Warnings, auto-moderation, tickets, and a comprehensive server log system.

🖥️Staff web dashboard

Server staff configure every module — panels, role mappings, logs — from a web dashboard without touching code.

Rank verification flow

티어 인증 흐름 — 라이엇 공식 로그인(RSO) 기반 소유권 검증.

Press “Tier Verification”

A member clicks the verification button on yeonubot's panel in Discord.

Sign in with Riot

The member signs in on Riot's official page (RSO). No password ever touches our system — we receive only the verified PUUID and Riot ID.

Tier lookup

The bot looks up the verified account's competitive tier — current act, previous act, and career peak.

Role assigned

The matching Discord tier role (Iron–Radiant) is assigned and kept up to date automatically as the player ranks up.

Ownership verification prevents impersonation — members can no longer claim accounts they do not own to obtain high-tier roles.

Data & privacy

최소 수집 원칙 — 자세한 내용은 처리방침 참조.
  • We store: Discord ID, PUUID, Riot ID (gameName#tagLine), and tier names.
  • We do not store: passwords, emails, or access tokens (tokens are used once at verification time and are not retained).
  • Non-commercial: no ads, no paid access, no monetization of player data.
